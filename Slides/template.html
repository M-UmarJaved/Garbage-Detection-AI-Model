SECTION 01
PROBLEM AND GOAL
WHY THIS MATTERS

SORT WASTE
IN REAL TIME.

Manual waste sorting is slow, inconsistent, and unsafe. Our goal is to detect and separate waste automatically using a compact vision system.

We trained a YOLO model on our custom dataset and deployed it on a live ESP32-CAM stream. The system decides movement commands in real time.

Output is monitored on a dashboard with logs and analytics for evaluation and future improvements.
